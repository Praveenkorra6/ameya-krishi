Our Story · మా గురించి

Rooted in the Soil,
Growing for the Farmer

రైతుల కోసం, ప్రకృతి కోసం

Founded by Jitta Bal Reddy, alongside his wife Jyothi Reddy and their daughter Ameya, Ameya Krishi Vikasa Kendram is more than a nursery — it is a living laboratory for sustainable, chemical-free agriculture in Bhongir, Yadadri Bhuvanagiri.

Jitta Bal Reddy, a former farm columnist for Telugu publications, spent years experimenting personally before building a center where hundreds of farmers visit every month to learn, observe, and source quality plants.

"We divide our farm work accordingly — everyone does their part."
— Jitta Bal Reddy

Featured by The Ecopreneur, BIGTV, V6 Teenmaar, and multiple Telugu agricultural channels, the center has become a recognized model of farmer-centric plant conservation and integrated farming in Telangana.

Our Approach · మా పద్ధతి

Farming in
Harmony with Nature

01
Natural Farming
ప్రకృతి వ్యవసాయం
Every crop, every method at Ameya Krishi is rooted in low-input, chemical-free agriculture. We demonstrate what sustainable farming looks like in practice — on real soil, in real conditions.
02
Agro-Homeopathy
అగ్రో-హోమియోపతి
We developed our own agro-homeo compositions through years of personal experimentation to address plant diseases caused by bacteria, fungi, and viruses — without chemicals. This work has been cited in academic research.
03
Plant Diversity Conservation
మొక్కల వైవిధ్య సంరక్షణ
We believe rare, indigenous, and farmer-centric plant varieties must be preserved, multiplied, and made accessible. Our 300+ variety nursery is built around this conviction.

A School for
Natural Farmers

The founder has announced plans to formalize Ameya Krishi's educational role with the opening of a dedicated farm school for enthusiastic natural farmers.

The school will build on the center's existing model of hands-on learning, agro-homeopathy training, and plant diversity education — making it accessible to a wider audience across Telangana and beyond.
